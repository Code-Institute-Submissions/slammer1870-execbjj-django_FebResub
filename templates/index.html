Meet the team

Meet the Team

Head Instructor
Sam Mc Nally

Rank: Black Belt

Age: 26

Sam is one of the top competitors in Europe winning medals in tournaments including the Pan American Championships and the NoGi European Championships.

He has been an instructor with ExecBJJ for the past 5 years as the head instructor of our Corporate Programmes.

With a wealth of experience he has coached people from complete beginners to competent practitioners.

Sam has an analytical approach to teaching with simplicity at the forefront of his instruction.
